## Research Profile

My research investigates the physical mechanisms that control chromatin accessibility. Using multiscale molecular simulations integrated with experimental data, I study how nucleosome dynamics, chromatin organization, and protein-protein interactions shape transcription factor search and activity in chromatin. A particular focus of my work is understanding how pioneer transcription factors access nucleosome-occupied DNA and how these mechanisms can be harnessed to improve cellular reprogramming.
